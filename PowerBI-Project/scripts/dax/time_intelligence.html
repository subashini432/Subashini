Time Intelligence DAX Measures

This Year Sales

This Year Sales =
CALCULATE(
    [Total Sales],
    YEAR(Sales[Order Date]) = YEAR(TODAY())
)

Last Year Sales

Last Year Sales =
CALCULATE(
    [Total Sales],
    YEAR(Sales[Order Date]) = YEAR(TODAY()) - 1
)

Sales Growth %

Sales Growth % =
DIVIDE(
    [This Year Sales] - [Last Year Sales],
    [Last Year Sales]
)

These measures compare current year vs previous year sales using DAX time-intelligence functions.
